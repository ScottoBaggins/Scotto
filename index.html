There and Back Again

An I.T Tale by Scotto Baggins

Concerning Hobbits

Hobbits are an unobtrusive but very ancient people, more numerous formaly than they are today; for they love aircraft models, drones and well kept server rooms: A well cooled and well cabled server room was their favourite haunt. Even in ancient days they were, as a rule, shy of 'the Big Folk', as they call us, and now they avoid us with dismay and are becoming hard to find.

Tongues began to wag in Hobbiton and Bywater; and rumour of the coming event travelled all over Rybrook. The history and character of Mr.Scotto Baggins became once again the chief topic of conversation; and the older folk suddenly found their reminiscences in welcome demand.
